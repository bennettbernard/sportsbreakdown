2021 Recap: They Can't Doubt You If You Don't Lose - Fairfield

"Come catch the spirit, Fairfield U"

Fairfield started their season one month later than most, but they played like they were in mid-season form. They opened the season with four dominant wins giving them the #12 power ranking at the beginning of April, 4-0. 8-0 after sweeping Iona. 10-0 after Quinnipiac. Fairfield shutout Rider 3 times during a 4-game series sweep, and they'd move up to #4 in the country and 14-0. They add two more wins and another 4 game sweep, this time vs #61 Monmouth NJ.

"For all to hear it, Fairfield U" "Red and White will always conquer"

After a 20-0 start, Fairfield was on top of the college baseball world, power ranked #1.

22-0 after 2 games vs St. Peters. 26-0 after Manhattan. Due to strength of schedule Fairfield would drop to #6, despite their 26-0 start.

In May, Fairfield would hit some roadbumps. On May 2nd, #251 Seina would upset #6 Fairfield, handing them their first loss of the year. Fairfield would lose two more games in May, but head into a regional featuring Southern University, Arizona St, and Texas as the #19 team in the country.

They'd go 1-1 vs #37 Arizona State, beat Southern University, but lose 2-12 vs #4 Texas on June 6th to end their season.

The two regional losses gave the power ranking major doubts about Fairfield and they'd end the season #105, with 1 win over a top 50 opponent and 4 losses to opponents outside the Top 100.

"All across the land we'll always stand by Fairfield U"

For one week, Fairfiend was mathematically the best team in baseball. In 2021, only five teams were able to see their name at the top of the power ranking: Oklahoma State, Vanderbilt, Arkansas, Oregon, and FAIRFIELD U.
